All about the Diptanuj

I’m currently in my 4th semester.

I’m mostly interested in Physics (like almost everybody here). I find simulations of physical phenomena using computational methods very interesting, and I’m currently trying to learn more about how such methods are used in Physics. I also like bubbles! Here is work I did on them.
